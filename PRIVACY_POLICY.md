# Privacy Policy — Cronada

**Last updated:** March 28, 2026

## Introduction

Cronada ("the App") is developed by EgeaINC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device.

### What the App does NOT do:
- Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Does not use cookies or similar technologies

### Local Storage Only

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- **Swimmers:** Names and skill levels of swimmers you register (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Groups:** Swimmer group assignments (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Sessions:** Training and competition timing records (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Pools:** Pool configurations you create (stored in local SQLite database)
- **Preferences:** App settings like language, theme, sound, and haptic feedback options (stored in SharedPreferences)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

### Data Sharing (User-Initiated Only)

The App allows you to **voluntarily** share data through:

1. **Export PDF/CSV:** You can export session results as PDF or CSV files. These files are generated locally and shared only through the share method you choose (WhatsApp, email, etc.).
2. **Share swimmers:** You can share swimmer data via a link. The data is encoded in the link itself — no server is involved.

These actions are always initiated by you and no data is sent automatically.

### Internet Usage

The App connects to the internet solely for:

1. **In-app purchases:** Processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

### Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:
- **Internet:** Required for in-app purchases through Google Play.
- **Vibration:** Used for haptic feedback on timer buttons (can be disabled in settings).
- **Foreground service:** Required to keep the timer running accurately when the screen is off.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. While coaches may register swimmer names for timing purposes, this data is stored locally on the coach's device only and is never transmitted.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
